Non Profit Organization Level

NPO Level Membership

With this organization level you get acces to every basic feature of the chamber, including participation in events, basic assesoring and a place in the website.
All for free only for registered Non Profit Organizations.

Bronze Level

Bronze Level Membership

With this organization level you get acces to every basic feature of the chamber, including participation in events, basic assesoring and a place in the website.
All for just a monthly payment of $9.99, special for starting or small businesses

Silver Level

Silver Level Membership

With this organization level you get acces to every basic feature of the chamber, and some premium features as discounts, premium placement in the website and events with major participation possibilities.
All for a monthly payment of $19.99, designed for Medium businesses and successful stores.

Gold Level

Gold Level Membership

The highest level of organization in the city, you will have VIP participation in events, having major advertisement during Events and being one of the first organization poeple will see in the website.
All for a monthly payment of $39.99, for big and multinational businesses.
